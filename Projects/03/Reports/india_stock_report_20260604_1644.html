Key Insight

The scan finds candidates -- not multibaggers. The biggest winners usually emerge from a combination of: earnings growth above 20-25%, large addressable market, strong management execution, multi-year industry tailwinds, and reasonable valuation at entry. Those qualitative factors often matter as much as the screen itself.
