How the ranking works: we don't sort by stars alone. Each item gets a confidence-weighted score (the same principle IMDb uses): a 4.6★ place with 2,000 reviews ranks above a 5.0★ place with 3 reviews. The more reviews an item has, the more its own rating counts; items with few reviews are pulled toward the average.
