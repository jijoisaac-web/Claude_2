No promoted listings, no pay-to-rank. Pulled straight from employer ATS systems, scored by how new the posting is and how well it matches what you searched. Germany, Austria & Switzerland + remote-Europe, to start.

What JobScout does differently

Big boards show you everything and make you dig — with paid listings shoved to the top regardless of fit. We rank a short list by two things we can actually measure honestly: how fresh a posting is, and how well it matches you.

🚫

Zero pay-to-rank

No listing can buy its way up. Every posting is scored by the same formula — freshness and match quality, nothing else.

🕒

Freshness over noise

Pulled directly from employer ATS systems rather than scraped boards, so stale reposts and abandoned "ghost" listings are far less common to begin with.

🔍

Honest about what we don't know

We don't have real applicant-count data, so we don't fake a "low competition" score. What you see is what we can actually measure — nothing invented.

🧭

A short list, not a firehose

A ranked top 24, not infinite scroll. Find something worth applying to, then go apply — not stay on the site.

How ranking works: opportunity score = 55% freshness (100 at day 0, decaying to 0 by day 21) + 45% relevance (title match scores highest, then tag match, then description match). No applicant-count or "competition" data exists in any free source we use, so we don't estimate or claim one — only the "New" badge (posted within 48 hours) is shown, since that's something we can verify directly.
