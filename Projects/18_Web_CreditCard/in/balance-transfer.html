📉 Low Interest / Balance Transfer Cards — India

Long low- or 0%-interest periods for paying down existing debt.

SBI Balance Transfer Plan

SBI Card
Annual fee: Processing fee ~2% (or ₹0 on longer plan) Existing SBI cardholders, Good–Excellent

Rewards: No rewards — a debt-repayment plan for existing credit card balances

Welcome offerNone
Intro rate0% for 60 days (2% processing fee) or 1.7%/month for 6 months (no processing fee)
Regular APR1.7%–3.5% per month depending on plan
  • Two plan choices let you trade off fee vs. repayment window
  • Meaningfully cheaper than standard 3.5%+/month revolving interest
Keep in mind:
  • Still accrues interest after the 60-day 0% window if unpaid
  • Only available to transfer balances onto an SBI Card
Verified July 2026

HDFC Bank
