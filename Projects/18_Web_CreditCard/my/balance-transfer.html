📉 Low Interest / Balance Transfer Cards — Malaysia

Long low- or 0%-interest periods for paying down existing debt.

Maybank Balance Transfer / -i

Maybank
Annual fee: Processing fee per promotion (sometimes RM0) Existing Maybank cardholders, Good

Rewards: No rewards — a debt-repayment plan for existing card balances

Welcome offerNone
Intro rate0% interest/profit rate for 12 months under current promotion
Regular APRReverts to standard card APR after the plan ends
  • 0% for a full 12 months under the current promotional plan
  • Sometimes offered with no upfront processing fee
Keep in mind:
  • Promotional terms change — confirm current offer before applying
  • Only available to transfer onto a Maybank card
Verified July 2026

CIMB Balance Transfer Plan

CIMB Bank
Annual fee: Processing fee applies Existing CIMB cardholders, Good–Excellent

Rewards: No rewards — installment-based debt consolidation

Welcome offerNone
Intro rateFrom 4.38% p.a. on amounts up to RM100,000
Regular APRFixed installment rate, repayable up to 5 years
  • Notably low headline rate versus typical revolving APR (~15–18% p.a.)
  • Long repayment tenure up to 5 years for large balances
Keep in mind:
  • Only available to CIMB cardholders
  • Longer tenure means more total interest paid even at a low rate
Verified July 2026

BSN Balance Transfer Plans

Bank Simpanan Nasional
Annual fee: Processing fee applies to longer plans Existing BSN cardholders, Good

Rewards: No rewards — a tiered debt-repayment plan

Welcome offerNone
Intro rate0% interest on the two shortest plans; competitive rates on 6–48 month plans
Regular APRVaries by plan length (6 to 48 months)
