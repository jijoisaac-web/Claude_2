Pro tips for getting the best deal

  • Check price history before buying — many "deals" aren't real discounts. Use the price-compare site listed for your country.
  • On Facebook Marketplace, sort by "Date listed: Newest first" and message quickly — good deals go in hours.
  • Search Instagram hashtags like #sale or #dealoftheday plus your product to find small-shop promos.
  • Add items to your cart and wait 24–48 hours — many stores email a discount code to recover the sale.
  • Compare refurbished/open-box listings — often 30–50% cheaper with warranty.
